Chief Executive of Alphabet

  • Sundar Pichai and the full name is Pichai Sundarajan who was born July 12, 1972, Madras which is now Chennai Tamil Nadu, India.

  • As a boy Sundar Pichai lived in Madras with his family. His father was an electrical engineer at the British multinational GEC, and always wanted the boys to get a good education. From an early age Sundar showed interest in technology and brilliant memory skills, especially while memorizing telephone numbers and other digit worthy things.
  • After earning a degree in metallurgy - B.Tech 1993 and a silver medal at the IIT - Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, he was awarded a scholarship to study at the Stanford University (M.S. in engineering and materials science in 1995.
  • He remained in the United States thereafter working briefly for Applied Materials and then earning an M.B.A -2002 from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
  • Sundhar Pichai joined Google in 2004 as the head of product management and development.
  • He started working on Google Toolbar which later enabled the use of Internet explorer and mozilla firefox web browsers and we got the easily accessible Google search engine.
  • Over the course of work he released the same in the year 2008. In the same year Pichai was named vice-president of product development and he became more active in the public role of Google.
  • His full name is Pichai Sundaranjan, he was born on June 10, 1972. He was born to Regunatha Pichai and mother Lakshmi Pichai. Sundar is married to his IIT batch mate Anjali Pichai. He has two lovely children Kiran and Kavya pichai. He is an American who was born in India.
